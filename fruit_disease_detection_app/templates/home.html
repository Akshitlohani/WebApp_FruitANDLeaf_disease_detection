Application Instructions

To utilize the disease detection feature on FruitWise, follow these steps:


1. Open the website and navigate to the disease detection section.

2. Choose the fruit from the available options among those listed with diseases.

3. Once selected, upload an image showing the affected part of the fruit plant.

4. Click on the submit button to initiate the disease detection process.

5. After the disease is detected, you can explore further details by visiting the disease encyclopedia section.

6. In the disease encyclopedia, you'll find product recommendations and preventive measures, aiding in understanding and addressing the plant's disease effectively.

Frequently asked questions

How often should fruit disease detection be performed?

The frequency of fruit disease detection depends on various factors, including the type of fruit, prevailing weather conditions, and the stage of crop growth. Generally, regular monitoring throughout the growing season is recommended to catch diseases early. Some growers may choose to conduct weekly or bi-weekly inspections during critical periods of fruit development.

Can fruit disease detection technology be used for preventive measures?

Yes, fruit disease detection technology is invaluable for preventive measures. By detecting diseases early, growers can take proactive steps to prevent their spread, such as adjusting irrigation practices, improving air circulation, applying appropriate fungicides or bactericides preventively, and implementing sanitation measures in the orchard or field.

Do I need to pay to access fruit disease detection services on your platform?

No, our platform is completely free to use. All you need to do is sign up for an account to gain access to our comprehensive fruit disease detection services. Enjoy hassle-free monitoring and protection for your crops without any cost involved.

Which fruits can I detect diseases for on your platform?

Our platform offers disease detection services for a variety of fruits, including tomato, orange, papaya, dragon fruit, and pomegranate. Whether you're growing one or multiple types of these fruits, our system is equipped to help you monitor and manage diseases effectively.

Is fruit disease detection technology easy to use for growers?

Yes, most fruit disease detection technologies are designed to be user-friendly and accessible to growers of all levels of expertise. Many systems offer intuitive interfaces and straightforward instructions, allowing growers to easily integrate disease detection into their regular farming practices.

What types of diseases can be detected using this technology?

Fruit disease detection technology can identify a wide range of diseases that affect fruits, including fungal infections, bacterial diseases, viral infections, and other common ailments specific to each fruit type. Examples include citrus greening in oranges, bacterial blight in tomatoes, and powdery mildew in papayas.
