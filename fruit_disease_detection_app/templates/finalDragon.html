1.Sanitation Practices: Ensure proper hygiene and cleanliness in the dragonfruit plantation. Remove and dispose of any infected or rotting plant parts promptly to prevent the spread of the disease.
2. Proper Irrigation: Avoid overwatering dragonfruit plants as excess moisture can create favorable conditions for bacterial soft rot. Use drip irrigation or water at the base of the plant to minimize water contact with the fruit and stems.

4. Copper-based Fungicides: Apply copper-based fungicides according to manufacturer's instructions as a preventive measure against bacterial soft rot. These fungicides can help suppress bacterial growth and protect the plants from infection.
